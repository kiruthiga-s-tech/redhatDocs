Introduction

Google Pay is a digital wallet and an online payment system developed by Google to power in-app and tap-to-pay purchases on mobile devices. This app enables you to send or receive money using Android phones, tablets, or watches to friends and family.


Prerequisites

You will need an internet connection, a bank account, and a phone number.


Request Money

  1. Open Google Pay.
  2. Select the contact to initiate the payment request.
  3. Enter the amount and description.
  4. Tap Request.

Note: You will receive notification when the request is accepted or declined.


Send Money

  1. Open Google Pay.
  2. Naviagate to Payments, select the person's name, phone number, account number, or UPI ID.
  3. Tap Pay.
  4. Enter the transaction amount and description.
