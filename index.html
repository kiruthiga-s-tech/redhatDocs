How to use Google Pay?

Introduction

Google Pay is a digital wallet and an online payment system developed by Google to power in-app and tap-to-pay purchases on mobile devices.
This app enables you to send or receive money using Android phones, tablets, or watches to friends and family.


Prerequisites

You will need an internet connection, a google account, a bank account, and a phone number.


Set up Google Pay

To set up Google Pay app on your device, follow the steps:

  1. Download the Google Pay app from the Google Play Store or the App Store.
  2. Enter the Phone number.
  3. Sign into your google account and add a payment method.

Request Money

To request money using Google Pay, follow the steps:

  1. Open Google Pay.
  2. Select the contact to initiate the payment request.
  3. Enter the amount and description.
  4. Tap Request.

Note: You will receive notification when the request is accepted or declined.


Send Money

To send money using Google Pay, follow the steps:

  1. Open Google Pay.
  2. Navigate to Payments, select the person's name, phone number, account number, or UPI ID.
  3. Tap Pay.
  4. Enter the transaction amount and description.
  5. Tap Proceed to pay.
  6. Enter UPI PIN.

Note: Ensure to enter the correct details. Once the transaction is complete, it cannot be reversed.
